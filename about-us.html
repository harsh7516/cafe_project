About Corner-Cup

Discover our passion for perfect brews and community gatherings.

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by coffee enthusiasts Alex and Maria Thompson, Brew Haven began as a humble venture in a vibrant city spot. Our journey transformed a simple idea into a sanctuary where exceptional coffee meets lasting friendships.

We proudly source beans from responsible farms in Colombia and Ethiopia, championing sustainable practices. Our expert roasters craft an exquisite range, from robust espressos to velvety lattes and invigorating cold brews.

Coffee is more than just a beverage here—it's a bridge. Explore our artful lattes or enjoy serene moments with a classic cappuccino, creating cherished experiences together.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To deliver unparalleled coffee that energizes, strengthens community ties, and upholds ethical sourcing for a better world.

Our Vision

To be the leading coffee destination known for inspiring moments, sustainable practices, and a welcoming atmosphere that brings people together worldwide.
